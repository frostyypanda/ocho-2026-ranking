How scoring works

Higher is better. Three components sum to the total:

  • Position (P) — per participant: max(0, 10 − Δ) reward, minus max(0, Δ − 8)² / 2 penalty. Exact rank = +10. Misses beyond 8 positions get a fast-growing penalty.
  • Medal tier (M) — per participant, compare your tier (using your cutoffs) vs actual tier (using actual cutoffs): same medal tier +20, both medals but different tier +8, both "no medal" +2, medal vs none mismatch 0.
  • Cutoff lines (C) — for each of gold/silver/bronze: max(0, 10 − 2·|Δcutoff|). Max +30.

Reference: a perfect prediction with cutoffs 3/6/9 scores 480. Random rankings hover around 60–120.
